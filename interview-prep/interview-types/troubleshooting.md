# Troubleshooting Interview Notes

## Purpose
This file helps with incident, performance, and root-cause interview questions.

---

## Strong Troubleshooting Pattern
1. stabilize service impact
2. gather evidence from logs, metrics, traces, and recent changes
3. isolate the failing component or dependency
4. validate the root cause
5. implement and verify the fix
6. add prevention measures

---

## Sample Answer
I approach troubleshooting systematically. I separate immediate mitigation from root-cause analysis, use evidence rather than guesswork, and make sure the incident results in long-term improvements such as better observability, release controls, or resilience patterns.
