# HR Interview Notes

## Purpose
This file prepares you for motivation, transition, role-fit, and compensation-adjacent questions.

---

## Typical Questions
- Tell me about yourself.
- Why are you looking for a change?
- Why this role or company?
- What are your strengths?
- What kind of environment helps you do your best work?

---

## Guidance
Keep answers positive, future-focused, and aligned to senior architect value. Avoid sounding transactional or overly generic.
