# Scenario-Based Interview Notes

## Purpose
This file stores architecture and leadership scenarios that require structured situational responses.

---

## Example Scenarios
- a legacy system must move to cloud with limited downtime
- multiple teams disagree on target architecture
- a critical platform must support rapid growth in six months
- a GenAI use case is requested with unclear risk controls

---

## Response Pattern
- clarify the problem
- identify risks and constraints
- present options
- choose a recommendation with trade-offs
- explain rollout and governance approach
