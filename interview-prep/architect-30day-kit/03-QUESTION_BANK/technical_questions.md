# Technical Question Bank (Senior Architect)

Use this as daily speaking practice. Aim for 2-4 minute structured responses.

## Azure and Cloud Architecture

1. Design a multi-region architecture for a global B2B SaaS on Azure.
2. How do you choose between AKS, App Service, and Container Apps?
3. Explain your approach to landing zone design for a large enterprise.
4. How do you architect cost optimization without compromising reliability?
5. What is your strategy for DR and business continuity across critical tiers?

## Microservices and Integration

1. How do you decide service boundaries in a domain with legacy coupling?
2. Explain saga orchestration vs choreography with a real example.
3. How do you enforce backward compatibility across distributed APIs?
4. What patterns do you use to ensure event processing reliability?
5. How do you avoid turning microservices into a distributed monolith?

## Security and Governance

1. Design Zero Trust for a hybrid enterprise application portfolio.
2. How do you implement least privilege at scale across many teams?
3. What APIM policies are mandatory in enterprise-grade APIs?
4. How would you secure service-to-service communication in Azure?
5. Describe your security review process in architecture governance.

## AI / GenAI / RAG

1. Design an enterprise RAG platform for internal knowledge assistance.
2. How do you evaluate and reduce hallucinations in production systems?
3. What are the top risks in GenAI adoption and your mitigations?
4. How do you design data isolation for multi-tenant AI workloads?
5. How do you balance model quality, latency, and cost?

## Data Architecture

1. Databricks vs Synapse vs Fabric: when and why?
2. How do you design medallion architecture for a complex enterprise?
3. How do you enforce data quality and lineage end-to-end?
4. Explain CDC strategy for modernization of legacy transaction systems.
5. How do you build data products for both analytics and AI?

## Migration and Modernization

1. How do you structure a cloud migration factory for 200+ apps?
2. Explain your strategy for AWS-to-Azure migration.
3. What are the common failure patterns in migration programs?
4. How do you sequence modernization waves to reduce business risk?
5. How do you measure migration success beyond "apps moved"?
