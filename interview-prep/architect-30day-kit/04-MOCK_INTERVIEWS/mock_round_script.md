# Mock Interview Script Pack

Use a timer. Record responses. Review for clarity, depth, and structure.

## Mock 1: Architecture Panel (90 min)

- 10 min: Background and leadership narrative
- 30 min: System design case
- 20 min: Security and governance drill
- 20 min: Migration strategy deep dive
- 10 min: Q&A and closing

Case prompt:
"Design a cloud-native modernization architecture for a global enterprise with legacy .NET systems, strict compliance requirements, and an AI roadmap."

Expected coverage:
- Target-state architecture and transition roadmap
- Platform and governance model
- Reliability, security, and cost controls
- Program risks and phased delivery

## Mock 2: AI/GenAI + Data Architecture (60 min)

- 15 min: RAG architecture design
- 15 min: Data platform strategy (Databricks/Synapse/Fabric)
- 15 min: Responsible AI and security controls
- 15 min: Cost and operationalization model

Case prompt:
"Design an enterprise knowledge assistant with secure retrieval, low hallucination risk, and clear ROI."

## Mock 3: Leadership and Stakeholder Interview (45 min)

- 5 min: Introduction
- 30 min: Behavioral and conflict scenarios
- 10 min: Vision and first-90-day plan

Focus metrics:
- Executive clarity
- Decision ownership
- Conflict resolution maturity
- Business outcome orientation
