# Architecture Governance Interview Notes

## Purpose
Notes for standards, review boards, design authority, and lightweight governance models.

---

## Core Themes
- guardrails over gatekeeping
- standards that improve consistency and security
- ADRs and review checkpoints
- balancing platform control with team autonomy

---

## Sample Architect Answer
I believe architecture governance should create consistency and reduce risk without becoming a delivery bottleneck. The best governance models are practical, transparent, and supported by reusable standards and automation.
