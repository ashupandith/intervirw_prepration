# Conflict Resolution Interview Notes

## Purpose
Notes for handling disagreements professionally in senior leadership and architecture scenarios.

---

## Sample Architect Answer
When conflict arises, I focus first on understanding whether the disagreement is about goals, constraints, or assumptions. I try to depersonalize the discussion, make the trade-offs visible, and move the group toward a decision that best serves the business outcome.
