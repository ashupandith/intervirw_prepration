# Delivery Trade-Offs Interview Notes

## Purpose
This file helps prepare for questions about balancing scope, quality, speed, and cost.

---

## Common Trade-Off Areas
- speed vs robustness
- technical debt vs immediate release pressure
- standardization vs local optimization
- platform investment vs short-term delivery

---

## Sample Architect Answer
I make trade-offs explicit and tied to business priorities. My goal is to avoid hidden compromise by showing what we gain, what we accept, and how we reduce the downside through phased delivery or clear follow-up actions.
