# Stakeholder Management Interview Notes

## Purpose
Interview prep for handling stakeholders across business, engineering, security, and delivery teams.

---

## What Good Answers Show
- listening and alignment
- clear communication of trade-offs
- expectation management
- influence without unnecessary escalation

---

## Sample Architect Answer
I manage stakeholders by first understanding their real priorities, then translating technical decisions into business impact. My aim is to create alignment through clarity and trust, especially when teams have different success criteria.
