# Fresh Start: How To Study (Concept-First)

This track is designed for "start from basics, grow to architect depth."

## Study Method (Daily 90-120 min)

1. **Learn** (30-40 min): Read one concept section.
2. **Explain** (20-30 min): Say it in your own words without notes.
3. **Apply** (20-30 min): Answer scenario questions.
4. **Recall** (10-20 min): Write 5 key points from memory.

## Concept Mastery Levels

- **L1 Basic:** What is it? Why needed?
- **L2 Working:** How to configure/implement?
- **L3 Architect:** When to choose it? Trade-offs?
- **L4 Enterprise:** Governance, cost, security, operations impact.

## How Interviewers Drill Deep

They usually go one concept at a time:
- Definition -> practical use -> limitations -> alternatives -> production failure cases

Prepare in the same order for every concept.

## Answer Template (Use Everywhere)

1. What it is
2. Where it fits
3. Why it is used
4. How to implement
5. Risks/pitfalls
6. Trade-offs and alternatives
7. Production best practices
