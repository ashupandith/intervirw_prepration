# Behavioral and Leadership (Concept Deep Dive)

## Concept 1: Influence Without Authority

At architect level, you often lead across teams without direct reporting lines.

### What interviewers look for
- Stakeholder alignment
- Decision facilitation
- Conflict resolution maturity

## Concept 2: Decision Governance

Use structured decision process:
- Problem definition
- Options and trade-offs
- Recommendation and rationale
- Decision record and accountability

## Concept 3: Delivery Under Constraint

Show how you balance:
- Scope
- Timeline
- Risk
- Quality
- Cost

## Concept 4: Conflict Resolution

Demonstrate:
- Listening to technical and business concerns
- Reframing debate around outcomes
- Escalating only when needed with clarity

## Concept 5: Mentoring and Team Growth

Senior architect interviews test if you scale through people.

### Good evidence
- Coaching plan
- Standards and playbooks
- Delegation model
- Succession and capability building

## Concept Questions (Practice)

1. Tell me about an architecture decision you influenced without authority.
2. Tell me about a major disagreement and how you resolved it.
3. Tell me about a failure and what changed afterward.
4. How do you align engineering and business leaders?
5. How do you mentor senior engineers into architect roles?
