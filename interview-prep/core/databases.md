# Databases Interview Notes

## Purpose
This file covers database choice, consistency, scale, and operational trade-offs.

---

## Core Topics
- relational vs NoSQL selection
- consistency vs scalability
- partitioning and indexing
- transactional boundaries
- read vs write optimization

---

## Sample Architect Answer
I choose the database based on access patterns, consistency needs, data relationships, scalability requirements, and operational fit. I do not start with the product name. I start with the workload characteristics and business risk of incorrect or delayed data.
