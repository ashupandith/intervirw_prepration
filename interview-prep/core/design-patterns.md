# Design Patterns Interview Notes

## Purpose
This file captures practical patterns worth mentioning in architect interviews.

---

## Patterns to Revise
- circuit breaker
- retry with backoff
- bulkhead isolation
- cache-aside
- publish-subscribe
- outbox pattern
- saga / compensation
- strangler pattern for modernization

---

## Interview Guidance
Do not just define patterns. Explain:
- the problem they solve
- the trade-offs they introduce
- how they apply in enterprise architecture

---

## Sample Answer
I use patterns when they solve a clear problem such as resilience, decoupling, or safe modernization. I avoid adding them only because they are well known, since every pattern also adds complexity and operational overhead.
